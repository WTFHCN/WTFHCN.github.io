3 HDU6715

$
证： \mu(lcm(i,j))=\mu(i)\times\mu(j)\times\mu(gcd(i,j))
$

$
\because i|lcm(i,j),
j|lcm(i,j),
gcd(i,j)|lcm(i,j)
$

$
\therefore \mu(i)=0\ or\ \mu(j)=0 \ or\ \mu(gcd(i,j))=0
$

$
\therefore \mu(lcm(i,j)) = 0
$

当都不为0的时候
$
\because K=k_{i}+{k_{j}}-{k_{gcd(i,j)}},
\mu(k)=(-1)^k
$

根据$(1+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{3}….\frac{1}{n})*n=nlog(n)$
所以只需要预处理前三项，就好了
